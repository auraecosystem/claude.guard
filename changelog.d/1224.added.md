- The idle-session GC pass (every launch, and `claude-guard gc`) now archives-then-removes **ephemeral** stacks that are idle past `CLAUDE_GUARD_SESSION_TTL` and have no live launcher attached — a time-based backstop for orphaned ephemeral sessions the dead-launcher reaper misses when the OS reuses a launcher's PID. Disable with `CLAUDE_NO_IDLE_GC=1`.
